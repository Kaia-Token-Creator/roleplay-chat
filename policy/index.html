Privacy & Refund Policy

Effective: Jan 21, 2026
No-login • Tab-scoped session • Transparent payments

This page explains what information is used when you visit Roleplay-chat.com, how third-party services may process data, and how refunds work for the 'Uncensored mode' one-time unlock.

This is a practical, plain-English policy. If you have questions, email showmetheaitools@gmail.com.

1) What we are (and what we are not)

Roleplay-chat.com is an anonymous, no-login AI roleplay chat. Your character and conversation are designed to stay available only for the lifetime of your browser tab while it remains open. We do not offer user accounts, profile pages, or “social” features. For your privacy, your information and conversations are not stored on our servers.

2) Information you provide

You may type character details (name, personality, scenario, etc.) and messages. You may optionally upload a profile photo for your character. These inputs are used to generate roleplay responses and to render your chat UI.

  • Character inputs: name, language, personality, scenario, optional avatar, and any optional traits you enter.
  • Chat messages: the text you send in chat.
  • Payment selection: if you choose 'Uncensored mode', you complete a PayPal checkout to unlock that mode.

3) Where your chat data lives

By default, your session is kept inside your browser tab. The site may use browser storage (for example, session storage) to remember your character and chat history within that tab. If you close the tab, the conversation may be lost.

Tip: If you want to preserve something, copy it to your own notes before closing the tab.

4) Cookies, analytics, and ads

We may use third-party services to understand traffic and to display ads. These providers may set cookies or use similar technologies according to their own policies.

  • Google Analytics may collect usage data (pages viewed, approximate location, device/browser info) to help us improve the site.
  • Google AdSense may use cookies or identifiers to show ads and measure performance.

You can control cookies in your browser settings. Depending on your region, you may also have additional opt-out choices.

5) Payments (PayPal) and what we receive

Payments for 'Uncensored mode' are processed by PayPal. We do not store your full payment card details on our servers. PayPal may provide us limited information needed to confirm the transaction (for example, a payment status and transaction identifiers).

  • Payment is a one-time unlock for 'Uncensored mode'.
  • Your statement may show a descriptor like “Playing game”.

6) Age requirement

The 'Uncensored mode' option is intended for adults only. If you are under 18, do not purchase or use 'Uncensored mode'. If you believe a minor used this feature, contact us at showmetheaitools@gmail.com.

7) How we use information

We use information to:

  • Provide and operate the chat experience (create characters, display messages, generate replies).
  • Prevent abuse, troubleshoot bugs, and keep the service reliable.
  • Measure site performance and improve UX (for example, via analytics).
  • Process purchases and confirm unlock status (via PayPal).

8) Data sharing

We do not collect your personal information. We don't know who you are. We share limited data only with service providers required to run the site, such as analytics, ads, and payment processors, and only to the extent necessary for those services to function.

9) Security

No website can promise perfect security, but we take reasonable measures to protect the service. You are responsible for keeping your device and browser secure.

10) Changes to this policy

We may update this policy to reflect product changes or legal requirements. The “Effective” date at the top will change when we do.

Refund Policy for payment

'Uncensored mode' is a digital, immediately-accessible unlock. Because access is granted right after successful payment, all sales are generally final, except where required by law.

  • Delivered as intended: If the unlock is delivered and you can access 'Uncensored mode' in your current tab, we do not typically offer refunds.
  • Non-delivery / technical issue: If you were charged but the unlock did not activate, contact us within 24 hours with your PayPal transaction details. We will investigate and, if confirmed, we may re-enable access or issue a refund.
  • Accidental tab closure / device reset: The service is designed to be tab-scoped. Closing the tab may end access for that session. Refunds are not provided for closing the tab, clearing browser data, switching devices, or similar user-side actions.
  • Chargebacks: If you initiate a chargeback without contacting us first, access may be blocked and we may be unable to restore service.

To request help: email showmetheaitools@gmail.com with your PayPal transaction ID and a brief description of what happened.

This policy is provided for transparency and user trust. It is not legal advice. If you need a region-specific policy (GDPR/UK GDPR/CCPA notices, cookie consent UI, etc.), you may want to consult a qualified professional.
